About Us

We operate where strategy, risk and human behavior meet.

Integrity Check Limited is a Kenya-based advisory firm specializing in risk management, governance, institutional strengthening and capacity development for public, private and development-sector organizations.

Established in 2012, the firm delivers practical, execution-focused work that strengthens institutional performance, decision-making discipline and operational resilience. We operate at the intersection of strategy, risk and human behavior — helping organizations move beyond compliance into genuine operational effectiveness.

We do not offer generic consulting. We deliver clarity in decision-making, practical risk solutions and measurable institutional impact.

Our Philosophy

Three convictions shape every engagement.

What most firms treat as paperwork, we treat as discipline. The distinction is the whole point.

01

Risk is a decision, not a document.

Risk is not a register filed and forgotten — it is a decision-making reality. We make it visible at the point where choices are actually made.

02

Governance is discipline, not structure.

Org charts and policies are not governance. Governance is discipline — consistently applied, owned by leadership, and lived day to day.

03

Training is behavior, not knowledge.

A workshop that changes nothing is a cost. Training succeeds only when it produces behavioral change that outlasts the session.
